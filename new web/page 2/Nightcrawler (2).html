Nightcrawler, a comprehensive review.

by Ivy Lu

Posted on July 20, 2017 at 1:00 PM

Can Jake Gyllenhaal make up for the incredible failure that the Prince of Persia was? The answer is yes, and fantastically so.

Nightcrawler starts off with a bang, depicting the life of night owl, Louis Bloom, in his frequent ventures: petty theft and assault.

The film takes a turn for the worse when Louis discovers his life of crime may not be necessary

After catching a news crew rushing to the scene of a crime only to film it and sell it to news channels, Louis becomes interested. He likes both the aspect of being self employed and witnessing grisly crimes.

We all know Louis is a psychopath, but his evilness evokes empathy in all audiences. We admire his wit and his work ethic. But poor Rick, though. He was my favorite character. R.I.P.
